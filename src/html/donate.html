Glory of Peace and Love is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization that makes all donations to us tax-deductible.

The best way to contribute to our efforts is to participate in our activities, but we also accept financial donations. By donating to Glory of Peace and Love, you are supporting our community-building efforts as well as our ongoing projects: protection of animals and support for disabled persons. We thank you for your support. If one or more of these projects is special to you, please tell us in the special instructions during payment. We will take that into account in utilizing your donation.
