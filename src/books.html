GOPAL Books

We are living in an era where information is available at the tip of our fingers anywhere and anytime, but the authenticity of the content and its source is questionable. Spiritual knowledge, especially, has always been imparted in the disciplic succession (Guru Parampara) from one generation to the next and it has been captured and made available to us in the form of books. The challenge we face today is where and how to find the right and authentic books which can quench our thirst and help us advance in our spiritual journey.

GOPAL inspired by Shreemaan Santosh Vaishnav Ji continuously works with authentic sources such as “Gitapress” to bring the right set of books to the community.
